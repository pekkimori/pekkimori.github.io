B. Featured "Now" card (recommended)

Hero → one full-width Now card showing current state (playing / reading / listening / obsessed-with) → uniform 2-wide grid of archives below. The Now card is the draw — feels alive, updated. The rest are reference. Natural hierarchy, easier to maintain.

C. Narrative sections

Three named "chapters" (e.g., what i make · what i'm into · worlds i return to) with 2–3 cards each. Adds rhythm and meaning. More editorial, more to maintain, risks feeling over-organized for a personal page.

B is my pick — the "Now" card solves a real UX problem (it's the thing visitors actually want) and the rest becomes low-maintenance reference.
